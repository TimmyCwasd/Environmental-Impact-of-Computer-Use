Environmental Impacts of computer use

Many steps of the process to use a computer affect the environment. Some key examples are production, use and disposal.

Environmental Consequences of Computer Production

A large amount of greenhouse gases are released every year as a result of computer prodution.

  • Harvesting the materials and minerals used for computer part production uses lots of energy and pollution due to harmful mining practises
  • Many parts such as the motherboard need high-precision lasers which consume a lot of energy and could be supplied by fossil fuels
  • As most parts are produced overseas, they need to be shipped by large cargo boats that produce 1000 megatons of carbon dioxide per year

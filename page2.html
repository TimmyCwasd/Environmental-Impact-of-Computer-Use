Environmental Impacts of computer use

Many steps of the process to use a computer affect the environment. Some key examples are production, use and disposal.

Environmental Consequences of Computer Production

Large amounts of greenhouse gases are released every year as a result of computer prodution. Research has found that just the manufacturing and transportation alone makes up 85% of the greenhouse gas emissions from a computer used for 6 years.

  • Harvesting the materials and minerals used for computer part production uses lots of energy and releases pollution due to harmful mining practises
  • Many parts such as the motherboard need high-precision lasers that consume a lot of energy which could be supplied by fossil fuels
  • As most parts are produced overseas, they need to be shipped by large cargo boats that produce around 1000 megatons of carbon dioxide per year
Every step of using a computer impacts the environment, from powering it to streaming content.
  • Watching short content on TikTok or texting your friends on Instagram take a lot of power, from transferring the data between servers to powering the router that allows you access to these apps
